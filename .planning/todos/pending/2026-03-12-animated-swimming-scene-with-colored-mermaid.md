## Problem

After coloring their mermaid, there's no payoff moment. Users should see their creation come to life in a light animated scene where the colored mermaid appears to swim with a moving background.

## Solution

After completing coloring, transition to an animated view showing the user's colored mermaid with a parallax or scrolling ocean/underwater background to simulate swimming. Keep it lightweight -- CSS animations or simple canvas movement rather than heavy animation libraries.
